About



My name is Jonathon Baker. I’m married and have 3 kids. I graduated from the Academy of Art of San Francisco with a BFA in in Illustration in 2002. I have been working in art related fields ever since from Freelance illustration to graphic design. I’ve even dabbled in tattoos and animation From time to time and have always been interested in learning web design and coding. My artistic ambitions are never satisfied.

I currently work as a graphic designer for Companion Systems. A company that builds the body/shell of ATM machines. I’m the one that places the bank logos and graphics on renderings for the banks to review.

In 2016 I was in an accident that changed the course of my life. I was in the hospital for three months and spent a year re-learning how to walk and move. I’ve learned many things from that experience, the most important being; the garbage truck always wins, especially if you’re on a bike.
